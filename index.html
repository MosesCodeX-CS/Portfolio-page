What I Bring to the Table

With a versatile skill set and a commitment to excellence, I offer comprehensive solutions tailored to your unique needs:

Custom Web Development

Building responsive, modern single-page applications (SPAs) and content management systems using React, Vue.js, Angular, or vanilla JavaScript. I focus on pixel-perfect designs, optimized performance, and seamless integration with back-end systems to meet your business goals.

Backend & API Design

Designing secure, scalable RESTful and GraphQL APIs with Node.js, Express, Laravel, Symfony, Django, or Flask. My solutions ensure robust server-side logic, efficient data handling, and smooth third-party integrations for enterprise-level reliability.

Database Architecture

Architecting and optimizing databases with MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Redis. I handle schema design, data migrations, indexing strategies, and performance tuning to ensure your data is secure, accessible, and lightning-fast.

Cloud & DevOps

Implementing CI/CD pipelines, containerization with Docker and Kubernetes, and cloud deployments on AWS, Azure, or GCP. I integrate monitoring tools like Prometheus and automate workflows to deliver scalable, resilient infrastructures.

Mobile App Development

Creating cross-platform mobile applications with React Native or Flutter, integrated with cloud backends. I prioritize intuitive UX, native-like performance, and compatibility across iOS and Android for a consistent user experience.

Security & Auditing

Conducting penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and ensuring OWASP Top 10 compliance and GDPR readiness. I safeguard your applications with encryption, secure authentication, and proactive risk mitigation strategies.
